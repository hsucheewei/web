TRENDING

Odette at National Gallery Singapore: Ranked 2nd in Asia's 50 Best Restaurants 2021

This two Michelin-starred restaurant is considered one of the best dining experiences on Earth.

1-Atico at ION Orchard: Panoramic View In The Heart Of Orchard

Coming alive at night, ATICO transforms into a radical futuristic playground. Visual compositions crowns the space, devoted to creating a surreal, immersive and psychedelic experience.

LAVO at Marina Bay Sands: Italian Restaurant and Rooftop Bar

Offering spectacular rooftop views of the city and a luxurious lounge, this award-winning Italian-American restaurant transforms into the perfect venue to take in the glow of the city at dusk.

USHIO Sumiyaki & Sake Bar at OUE Tower

Nestled within the grandeur structure of OUE Tower is USHIO. Its name inspired by the pristine water that separates its location and Marina Bayfront, translates to “tide”. In the same fashion, it sees the necessity to go on a relentless pursuit to ride the waves of opportunity and trends.

Dusk Restaurant & Bar at Mount Faber: European Tapas and Cocktails

At 100 metres above sea level surrounded by lush rainforest, Dusk Restaurant & Bar is the perfect vantage point for panoramic views of HarbourFront & Sentosa Island. From romantic date nights and casual get-togethers, Dusk is the perfect alfresco hotspot to be at.

VUE at OUE Bayfront: Stunning City Views and Fusion Food

Perched atop OUE Bayfront on Collyer Quay, VUE presents a chef-driven menu that emphasises binchotan-grilled specialities at the heart of its cuisine offerings, complemented by a versatile selection of wines. VUE is also home to Singapore's first rooftop spritz bar, further setting itself apart from other dining destinations around town.

CÉ LA VI at Marina Bay Sands: Rooftop Dining, Sky Bar and Lounge

Enjoy panoramic views at the top of MBS with exquisite dishes of the highest quality from the restaurant's menu which includes Grilled Fremantle Octopus in Taiwanese glaze, Black Truffle "Sushi Rice" Risotto, Salted Egg Tasmanian Salmon and many more Asian fusion dishes.
